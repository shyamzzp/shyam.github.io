    “Hearty Welcome to our CITO NIDHI LIMITED”


    CITO NIDHI LIMITED is a trusted financial Organization which was promoted and established by the Core Banking Professionals with 25+ Years of Banking Experience across all verticals. It’s a Quasi Banking Mutual Benefit Company with Co-Operative Principles registered under Section 406 of the Companies Act, 2013 (Section 620A of the Companies Act, 1956) with RBI Nidhi rules. It’s duly approved by the Govt. of India, Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA). A Nidhi is wholly or partially regulated by the RBI, and is enabled to issue directions to them in matters identifying with their deposit acknowledgment activities and Lending activities.

    CITO Nidhi is established with a motive of promoting self sustenance and social empowerment of the poor and downtrodden families and holding the social responsibility of Financial Inclusion. Performing through Banking system, as a Nidhi We accept deposits from our members/shareholders to stimulate the mutual growth of our members through Trust and Responsibility within the stipulated norms issued by the RBI/Ministry of Corporate Affairs. The funds mobilized are utilized to a great extent for advancing of Gold Loans, Property Loans, Small Business Loans, SME Loans, Group Loans and Loans against approved Govt. Securities to its members. With the strong banking background & experience of the promoters, we believe that success of business comes from creativity and team work with techno banking initiatives. Regulatory and CIN (corporate identification Number) of our CITO NIDHI Ltd., is U67100AP2019PLC111283.
