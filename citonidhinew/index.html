    CitoNidhi INDIA CitoNidhi LIMITED headquartered in Panipat (Haryana) Incorporated under company act 2013, under CitoNidhi Rules 2014, by the innovative leadership of inspired professional s in this revolutionary market trend. A company declared as a CitoNidhi (MBFC) under section 620(A) of the Companies Act,1956 . CitoNidhi have many features which make them stand distinctly apart from many other Non-Banking financial institutions who are working under the guide line of R.B.I

    CitoNidhi Companies Registration To start a CitoNidhi Company in India the first step is to incorporate a Limited Company, under the Companies Act, 2014. Hence, During incorporation of the CitoNidhi company, care must be taken to ensure that the object of the Limited Company mentioned in the Memorandum of Association is that of cultivating the habit of thrift and savings amongst its members, receiving deposits from, and lending to, its members only, for their mutual benefit.

    Depositors can choose from a wide range of deposit products with maturities ranging from 12 to 60 months at competitive rates of interest and with different features to suit the investment needs of individual Members.
